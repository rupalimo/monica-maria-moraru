Bio

‘The Behavior of Buildings’ (working title), takes as its underpinning the destructive Vrancea earthquake that occurred in Romania in 1977. Merging both documentary and speculative fiction, the film traces sinuous relations to disaster through a meditation on photography, mythology, sound, and resistance. The film employs a multi-channeled score to invoke mythical elements as custodians of material cultures, exploring how rubble and sand can evoke a narrative of living beyond the legacy of nation building.
